What Changes If These Pass?

Understanding the Process:

The discharge petition (H.Res. 581) is a procedural mechanism to force a floor vote. If successful, it would bring H.R. 4405 to the House floor for consideration. H.R. 4405 establishes:

  • A presumption of disclosure for all government-held Epstein records
  • Automatic redaction of minor victims' identities and re-identifying details
  • An independent review board including a survivor advocate
  • A public index documenting any withheld records and justifications
  • Firm deadlines for release (similar to the JFK Records Act model)

For detailed explanation of discharge procedures, see: ABC News Explainer
